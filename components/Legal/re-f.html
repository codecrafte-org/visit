Refund Policy

Project Kick-off (Advance)

Kaam shuru karne ke liye jo 50% advance payment li jati hai, wo non-refundable hai. Ye payment hamari team ke time aur resources reserve karne ke liye hoti hai.

Cancellation Policy

Agar aap project ke darmiyan kaam rokna chahte hain, toh jitna fees pay ki ja chuki hogi wo refund nahi hogi. Mazeed ye ke, jitna extra kaam ho chuka hoga, uske charges bhi client ko dene honge.

Exceptional Cases

Refund sirf aik soorat mein diya ja sakta hai: Agar CodeCrafte agreement ke mutabiq deliverables provide karne mein mukammal nakam ho jaye aur koi munasib hal na nikal sakay.

Post-Handover

Ek baar final files ya source code client ko handover kar diya jaye, uske baad koi bhi refund request qabil-e-qabool nahi hogi.
